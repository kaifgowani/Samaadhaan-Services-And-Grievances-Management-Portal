Software Application for Monitoring And Disposal, Handling of services and grievances in Atal Nagar

SAMADHAN - A Unified Citizen Portal

  • The purpose of the SAMADHAN project is to create a comprehensive software application called "SAMADHAN" (Software Application for Monitoring And Disposal, Handling of services and grievances in Atal Nagar) that serves as a one-stop solution portal for the City Municipal Corporation.
  • The project aims to enhance the quality of service delivery, improve citizen experience, and promote transparent and responsive governance through automated and streamlined service delivery.
  • Through this portal, citizens can conveniently perform tasks such as making payments for taxes and bills, raise grievances, and seek assistance with urban issues.
  • Also Citizen can lodge their Grievance 24x7 to the public authorities on any subject related to service delivery. The status of the grievance filed can be tracked with the unique registration ID provided at the time of registration of the complainant to user.
  • The application also facilitates the tracking of essential services, including garbage collection vehicles.
  • SAMADHAN is envisioned as a transformative effort to enhance the quality of service delivery, increase transparency in governance, and improve the overall experience of citizens when interacting with their municipal corporation.
